# NaughtyBot — Help

**Theme:** gambling addiction. Every sphere is a bet — sometimes you win big, sometimes the house takes everything.

---

## Controls

Same as Unreal’s Third Person template / character:

| Action | Keyboard & Mouse | Gamepad |
|--------|------------------|---------|
| Move | **W A S D** | Left stick |
| Look | **Mouse** | Right stick |
| Jump | **Space** | A / Cross |

---

## Magic balls (spheres)

Pick them up in the arena. Colors tell you the bet:

### Green — Heal
Restores your health. The safe chip — take it when you’re hurt.

### Orange — Destroy drone
A gamble on the drones:
- **Win:** a random drone is destroyed
- **Lose (chance):** a **new** drone spawns instead

### Blue — Ally
A gamble on your team:
- **Win:** spawn a new ally
- **Lose (chance):** a **random ally is destroyed** instead  

If that kill was your last ally, the round is over.

---

## Death & possession

When your current body dies, you **possess another living ally** (if any remain).  
You’re not out until the last pawn is gone — keep the streak alive.

---

## Falling floors

At the **start of a round**, floors can begin dropping. Stay moving. Standing still is how the house cashes you out.
